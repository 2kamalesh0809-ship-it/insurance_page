Frequently Asked Questions

What is a waiting period?

A waiting period is the time you must wait before certain benefits of your policy become active. Usually, it applies to pre-existing conditions.

How do I file a claim?

You can file a claim through our mobile app, website, or by calling our 24/7 claims helpline at 1-800-SFS-SAFE.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at the time of renewal or by contacting our customer service for a mid-term endorsement.
