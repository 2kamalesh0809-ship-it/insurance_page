Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Introduction

Welcome to SFS Insurance. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and services.

By accessing our platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We ensure that your data is handled in accordance with the latest data protection regulations in India.

Data Collection

We collect information that allows us to provide you with tailored insurance solutions. This includes:

  • Personal Identity: Name, age, gender, and government-issued IDs.
  • Contact Details: Email address, phone number, and residential address.
  • Vehicle/Health Records: RC copies, medical history, and pre-existing condition reports (where applicable).
  • Financial Information: Payment details and bank account information for claim settlements.

Usage of Data

The information we collect is used primarily to facilitate your insurance journey:

  • Generating accurate insurance quotations based on your risk profile.
  • Processing policy applications and instant digital issuance.
  • Assisting in fast-track claim settlements and verifications.
  • Improving our website experience through AI-powered analytics.
  • Sending critical policy updates and renewal reminders via SMS/WhatsApp.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us understand how you interact with our content and allow us to remember your preferences (like your city or vehicle type) for smoother future visits.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, though some features of our site may not function optimally if cookies are disabled.

Security

SFS Insurance employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmissions and store sensitive information in secure, firewalled servers.

Our payment gateway partners are PCI-DSS compliant, ensuring that your financial transactions are safe and encrypted.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access and review the personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The right to withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • The right to request the deletion of your account and personal data (subject to statutory record-keeping requirements).
