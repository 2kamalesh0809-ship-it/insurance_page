Simplified Claims Process

Our commitment is to be there when you need us most. Fast, transparent, and hassle-free claims.

How it Works

4 simple steps to settle your claim quickly.

1

Intimation

Notify us as soon as an event occurs via our app, website, or toll-free number. We'll register your claim immediately.

2

Document Submission

Upload scanned copies of required documents through our digital portal for faster processing.

3

Verification

Our dedicated claims officers will review your case and may conduct a quick verification if needed.

4

Settlement

Once approved, the claim amount is transferred directly to your bank account or paid to the hospital.
