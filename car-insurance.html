Decision Guide

How to choose the right
car insurance policy?

01

Coverage Type

Choose between third-party liability, comprehensive, or standalone personal protection based on your needs.

02

Vehicle Value

Ensure your coverage accurately matches your car's current market value (IDV) for proper protection.

03

Driving Style

Your driving habits and mileage significantly impact your premium costs and coverage requirements.

04

Budget

Select a balanced deductible and premium ratio that keeps you protected without overspending.

05

Location

Your primary driving location and city affects risk assessment and final policy pricing.

06

Discounts

Always check your eligibility for no-claim bonuses (NCB) and special corporate offers to save more.
