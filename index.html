What is SFS Policy?

With rising medical inflation, a single hospitalization can deplete years of savings. SFS Policy is designed to provide you with a robust financial safety net when you need it most. Our flagship plan offers comprehensive coverage for a wide range of medical expenses, including inpatient hospitalization, day-care procedures, and critical illness treatments.

Beyond just hospital bills, we provide flexibility with pre-designed add-ons, unlimited doctor consultations, advanced diagnostic testing, and emergency ambulance services. At SFS, we believe in proactive wellness, ensuring your family health is secured without the burden of financial stress.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is health insurance and why do I need it?

+

Health insurance helps cover medical expenses and protects you from high hospital costs.

What does this policy actually cover?

+

It covers hospitalization, treatments, doctor consultations, and selected medical expenses.

What is not covered in this insurance?

+

Pre-existing conditions (initial period), cosmetic treatments, and non-medical expenses are usually excluded.

How do I make a claim?

+

You can make a claim through cashless hospitals or submit bills for reimbursement.

How long does it take to get claim approval?

+

Most claims are processed within a few hours for cashless and a few days for reimbursement.

Is cashless treatment available in hospitals?

+

Yes, you can avail cashless treatment at our network hospitals.

How much premium do I need to pay?

+

Premium depends on your age, coverage amount, and selected plan.

Can I pay monthly instead of yearly?

+

Yes, flexible payment options like monthly and yearly are available.

Are there any hidden charges?

+

No, all charges are clearly mentioned upfront with full transparency.

Can I include my family in one plan?

+

Yes, family floater plans allow coverage for multiple members under one policy.

Is there any age limit to buy insurance?

+

Most plans are available for individuals from young adults to senior citizens.

How fast can I get my policy after applying?

+

Your policy can be issued within minutes after successful verification.

Can I talk to someone before buying?

+

Yes, you can connect with our experts via call or WhatsApp anytime.

Will someone help me during hospital admission?

+

Yes, our support team assists you throughout the hospitalization process.
